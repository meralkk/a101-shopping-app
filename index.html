Project Brief

A101 online shopping is an application that allows products such as food, beverage, cleaning products to be ordered online and delivered to the address.

The Problem

Discover the pain points of the current app and propose solutions to¬†improve user experience.

The Goal

The goal was to understand A101 Online Shopping users' response to the current mobile app, discover pain points, and propose some solutions to improve their experiences.

I prepared this study in line with Userspots' 6-week training. My role here is to prepare both UX and UI design.

I conducted tests with 3 people who did not use the application before, to measure the usability and comprehensibility of the product over scenarios I had previously determined. I set up 4 different scenarios in these tests.


Research questions:


  • Can the user easily find the product he/she is looking for?
  • Is the information given in the product details sufficient?
  • Is it easy to choose a product quantity?
  • Is it difficult to add to the cart?¬†
  • What kind of a path does he/she follow when searching for a product with a special offer?
  • Is it having difficulty completing the checkout step?
